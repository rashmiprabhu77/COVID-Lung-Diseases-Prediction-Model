            <h3>Our Objective</h3>
            <p>Lung disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide and early detection is crucial for effective treatment. However, traditional diagnostic methods can be time-consuming, invasive and often require specialized equipment. This project aims to leverage the advancements in computer vision to develop a novel approach for detecting lung disease using medical images. By utilizing deep learning algorithms, we aim to create an efficient, non-invasive and cost-effective tool that can assist healthcare professionals in the early detection of lung diseases. The goal of this project is to improve patient outcomes by providing a fast and accurate method for detecting lung disease at an early stage.</p>
